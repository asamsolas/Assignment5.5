Frequently Asked Questions

What is Affidavit?

Affidavit is a written statement confirmed by oath or affirmation, for use as evidence in court.

Why do i need affidavit?

You need an affidavit to affirm a claim that someone is making.

Can i use affidavit instead of birth Certificate

You may submit a sworn affidavit of birth as a legal substitute for birth

Does NYSC accept affidavit for change of name

No, The NYSC requires marriage certificate and a newspaper change-of-name publication to effect this change

How much is affidavit in Nigeria

The official charge for affidavit in Nigeria is #2,030.46
